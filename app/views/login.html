What is this?

We are a web app which allow jobseekers to connect directly with employees of many companies in order to facilitate their recruitment process.

Connect with your linkedin account, set up your search criterias, browse referrers or candidates profiles, use your credits to contact users or open contact requests. Repeat daily.

Linkedin Sync



Create a profile in one click which will always stay up to date and in phase with your actual linkedin profile.

It's free



You can use the app for free and increase your capacity to connect by referring friends to the app or subscribe to a premium account.

Anonymous Profile



No personal information will be displayed to others until you successfully connect with another user.

Why GoRefer.Me?

Today, 1 of every 2 employers offers a formal referral bonus program to their employees. The employees can refer external candidates to their HR department, and if the candidate is finally hired, the employee will earn benefit or cash bonus from its employers.

On average, only less than 10% of resume received by a HR department come from such referral. However it has been proved that they have the highest chance of success.

Who we wish to help?

Employees to spend no more than 10 efficient minutes everyday to find great candidates to refer and earn their Bonus Referral more frequently

Jobseekers to connect directly with employees of great companies and ask them : “Go Refer Me!”
